Showcase

These are some of the apps that are powered by Jikan one way or another!

Add your app
BetterMAL

Once upon a time there was a site called MAL. It was a good site but their "dev team" never updated the site. It looks like from 2007. I thought like "Well I can fix things".
MALStats 2

Web app for user list statistics
Seiyuu.moe

Did you ever wonder if those two seiyū worked together? This website allows you to select them from the list and find joint works between Japanese voice actors.
Simulcasts Online

Curb your plan to watch list with Simulcasts.online! We provide a daily and weekly schedule of airing animes, and list new releases daily!
Perpusindo.info

PerpusIndo is a website that contains a collection of download links from fansub or fanshare or individual in Indonesia.
Anime Index

Anime Index allows users to explore and track Anime with its details or add it to their offline database.
OtakuBook

This anime browser application is built on top of the JIKAN Rest API to enable to user to browse and search for different animes.
Openings.ninja

Openings.ninja allows you to watch all openings and endings from thousands of anime series and movies.
TaiYaKi

Watch your favorite anime ad-free and sync them with your MyAnimeList, available for iOS
MyWaifuList

Build a list of your best waifus
Random Anime Picker

MyAnimeList: Pick a random anime
Mal-it

Covers generator for MAL
MyOngoingsCalendar

App that allows you to add and manage airing anime in Japan
MyMAL

This is an unofficial MyAnimeList client that help you manage your anime and manga list with ease and discover new ones
Mangatic

Mangatic est un site web en mode SPA réalisé avec API Rest JIKAN. Ce site a pour but de chercher des différents anime
MyAnimeCloud

Extends MAL functionality for voice actors (seiyuu used)
MALClient

MALClient is 3rd party app for managing your anime and manga list on MyAnimeList.net as well as accessing other website features.
Animieru

Search for your favorite Anime, Manga, Characters, and Voice Actors!
Aruppi

Application to see all the information of your favorite anime and manga, listen to music, watch videos or read manga.
Nepgear Bot

Telegram bot that allows you to search for your favorite Anime, Manga, Character with other useful features !!!
Aiura Bot

Aiura is a bot you can use to pull up MAL profiles on Discord and also check out anime and manga information.
